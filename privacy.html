Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us, that's why we made this privacy policy document, to show you the data our program (Temp_Cleaner GUI) and this website collect about you.

Temp_Cleaner GUI (our program) collects the following data about you (and for the following purpose(s)):

Data collected Purpose(s)
Your IP Address (Your Public IP Address) (via the built-in Updater program) The Updater program sends your Public IP Address to GitHub in order to allow downloading files 'tcg.txt' and 'release_notes_tcg.txt' from this repository for showing to the user within the Updater program.
These files will be automatically downloaded and stored in the current installation folder of Temp_Cleaner GUI (the directory where Temp_Cleaner GUI's executable is stored in) and will be deleted automatically when the Updater program exits (When the user presses "Don't Update" or "Install Update" buttons)

This process is automated by the urllib.request library.
Basic Information about your OS (Windows or Linux) Volume (or disk drive)
These information are:
Your OS Disk type, Free disk space on your OS volume, Total Disk space on your OS volume
  • To show you how many disk space you have freed up during a cleaning session.
  • To provide you with the basic windows volume information this utility provides via the F2 hotkey.
The current logged in User Name For showing in the title of Temp_Cleaner GUI's Main Window.

Temp_Cleaner GUI's website (This website) collects the following data about you (and for the following purpose(s)):

Data collected Purpose(s)
Your IP Address (Your Public IP Address) This website (like any other website), Requires permission to obtain your Public IP Address for it's functionality.
  • You can't revoke access to your Public IP Address as it is required to make this website work on your PC.
Your payment method data and other information collected by other 3rd-party services Linked to this website Other services linked to this website may collect additional information about you, such as your payment method data and your public IP Address, These data will only be collected when you choose to provide them with, or when you use the other 3rd-party services linked with this website.
For more information, please read each 3rd-party service's privacy policy from the links available below:
  • buymeacoffee's Privacy Policy (the donation service)
    Your data will be processed in accordings with the privacy policy of this website only when you choose to donate.
  • GitHub's Privacy Policy (the service we use to host the code of Temp_Cleaner GUI)
    This is the service we use to host the official source code of Temp_Cleaner GUI, by clicking the button to download the source code of Temp_Cleaner GUI, GitHub will collect information about you.
Your PC's identification information, such as your OS Name, OS Version, Platform Role, and your geographical location (country only) These data must be collected about your PC when you go to the Official SourceForge Page of Temp_Cleaner GUI to download Temp_Cleaner GUI for your PC, and these data are only collected for the analytic purposes and will not be used for anything else.
