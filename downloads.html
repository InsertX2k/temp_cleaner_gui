Previous versions of Temp_Cleaner GUI (probably including this one) might be falsely detected as malware/PUP by many antivirus software, This happens because in previous versions I used UPX to compress the executable and other binary files in the program's files and this triggers many antivirus software.
You can add Temp_Cleaner GUI's folder to your Antivirus exclusions, Choose your Antivirus Software from these and learn how to add an exclusion in them:

  • Microsoft Defender (aka. Windows Security)
  • Avast Antivirus/Avast One
  • Avira Antivirus
  • MalwareBytes for Windows
  • McAfee Products

If your AV product is not listed here, simply head to Google, and search for "How to add a [Insert AV Product name here] antivirus exclusion"
